## Gaming vocabulary (allowed when context is clear)

duo, squad, party, lobby, queue, co-op, ranked, casual, cross-play

Do not imitate proprietary game UI or imply publisher partnerships.

## Copy snippets (homepage)

- **Tagline:** Find gaming friends who fit your life.
- **Headline:** Meet gamers you'll actually want to play with again.
- **Trust line:** Adults 18+ · Private connections · No swiping · No paid boosts

## Environment preference language

Use **interaction environment** framing, not identity labels:

- "Interruption-friendly" — patience with pauses, not proof of parenthood
- "Clean communication" — not "family gamer" or moral judgment
- "Alcohol-free gaming environment" — not recovery status inference
- "No hostility toward religion or nonreligion" — conduct boundary, not belief field

Users never disclose **why** they selected a boundary.
